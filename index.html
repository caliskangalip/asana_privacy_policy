Privacy Policy

Asana Math Logic Puzzle

This Privacy Policy describes how Asana Math Logic Puzzle ("the app") handles user data in compliance with Google Play User Data Policy and applicable privacy laws, including the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

About the App

Asana Math Logic Puzzle is a math-based logic puzzle game designed for general audiences. The game focuses on problem-solving and logical thinking.

Personal Data Collection

  • The app does not require user registration or login.
  • We do not collect, store, or share personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

Advertising & Third-Party Services

  • The app displays advertisements using Google AdMob.
  • AdMob may collect limited, non-personal, and anonymized data (such as device information and ad interaction data) to provide and improve ads.
  • Ads shown in the app are configured to comply with Google Play policies, including child-appropriate and non-personalized advertising when applicable.

Children’s Privacy (COPPA)

  • This app is suitable for a general audience and can be used by children.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13.
  • Any data collected by third-party advertising services is handled in accordance with COPPA and Google Families Policy requirements.

Permissions

  • The app itself does not directly use the READ_PHONE_STATE permission.
  • This permission may be automatically included by third-party SDKs such as advertising libraries.

Internet Connectivity

  • The app can be used offline.
  • An internet connection is required only for displaying ads.

Data Security

Since the app does not collect or store personal user data, there is no user data retained on our servers.
